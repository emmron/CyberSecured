What is a Cyber Security Audit?

A Cyber Security Audit is a Consultancy Service providing a detailed cyber review of a business’ IT infrastructure. Vulnerabilities, threats and risk get identified and classified in severity levels. An Executive Summary as well as a detailed remediation report will be provided outlining necessary and recommended mitigation steps.


An effective and secure cyber network supports the well functioning of a business, and protects its data, money and reputation. That is why adopting regular cyber security audits to analyse the effectiveness of their IT ifnrastructure is so important for businesses.
